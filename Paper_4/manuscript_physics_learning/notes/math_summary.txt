MATH SUMMARY (PAPER A)
======================
Core mathematical objects:
- Oracle target: theta*(x) = argmax_theta S(x, theta)
- Learned initialization: theta_hat_0 = f_phi(x)
- Semi-amortized refinement: theta_hat_{b+1} = R(x, theta_hat_b; B)

How the math is used:
- The paper does not derive a new optimizer.
- The equations are used to define the teacher, the learned predictor, and the refinement operator clearly enough for review.
- Geometry summaries are descriptive rather than theorem-driven: PCA variance explained, centroid displacement, and neighborhood agreement are treated as empirical evidence for structure.

Important interpretive guardrails:
- The argmax target is optimizer-defined, not biological ground truth.
- Geometry is used to support structured learnability, not to claim exact manifold identifiability.
- Refinement equations support the argument that learned initialization has operational value, not that amortization replaces optimization.
