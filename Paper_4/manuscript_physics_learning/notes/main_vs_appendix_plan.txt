MAIN VS APPENDIX PLAN (PAPER A)
================================
Paper identity:
- Main claim: the optimizer-derived parameterization is structured and learnable enough to justify amortized prediction plus semi-amortized refinement.
- Explicit non-claim: this paper is not the full-vs-patch deployment paper and not the calibration/routing paper.

Source-line accounting:
- integrated_full_vs_patch_manuscript.pdf: overlap only in shared refinement-budget and parameter-difficulty evidence.
- main_integrated_full_vs_patch_codex_v1.pdf: corroborative shadow line only; not a separate narrative source.
- main_speciesref_fixed-1-1.pdf: used only for selective-study ablations that clarify learnability limits.
- optimizer/manifold strengthened outputs: primary figure family and narrative center.

Main text curation:
- Intro and lineage table: explicit separation of the three prior manuscript lines.
- Geometry core: PCA, convex hulls, compact audited geometry summary.
- Geometry secondary: embedding consistency, mean displacement, PCA-3D, covariance ellipsoids, raw 3D surfaces.
- Learnability limits: per-parameter difficulty plus morphology-linked scatter plots.
- Semi-amortized value: refinement contraction and morphology-fidelity tables.
- Qualitative interpretation: leaf montages and representative optimization landscapes.

Appendix curation:
- Additional manifold diagnostics: centroid-shift groups, radar means, t-SNE, parallel-coordinate deltas, pairwise delta plots, density isosurfaces.
- Teacher-target distributions: optimizer score/runtime/status, area and contour histograms, parameter histograms.
- Extra morphology linkage: teacher-vs-predicted area fraction and threshold sweep.

Why this split works:
- Main text carries the paper's scientific claim and enough qualitative evidence to justify it.
- Appendix absorbs the broader extensive-study figure family without changing the paper into a runtime/deployment narrative.
