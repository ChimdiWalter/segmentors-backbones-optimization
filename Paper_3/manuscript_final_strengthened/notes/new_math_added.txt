NEW MATHEMATICS ADDED
======================

Location: Section III-C "Parameter-Cloud Geometry" (new subsection)

1. Parameter shift definition:
   delta_j = theta_j^ell - theta_j^g in R^7
   where theta^g is the globally optimized parameter vector and
   theta^ell is the locally re-optimized parameter vector for patch j.

2. Centroid shift vector:
   Delta_bar_theta = bar_theta^ell - bar_theta^g
   Magnitude ||Delta_bar_theta|| quantifies the systematic displacement
   of local solutions from global solutions in standardized units.

3. Covariance ellipsoids:
   Sigma^g, Sigma^ell define 2-sigma ellipsoids whose overlap
   measures the structural similarity of global and local solution sets.

4. k-NN classification agreement:
   For each point in the delta cloud, check if k=7 nearest neighbors
   share the same Delta-D group (local-better, neutral, local-worse).
   Agreement above random baseline (1/3) indicates predictive structure.

These definitions support the new parameter-cloud geometry results section
(Section V-G) and are consistent with the existing notation for theta,
theta*, and the search space Theta.
