Distinctness Notes — v2 Full Study
====================================
This paper is DISTINCT from the optimizer paper (Paper 3 existing):

1. DIFFERENT QUESTION: How to efficiently deploy physics inference
   (not how to build the optimizer/objective).

2. DIFFERENT METHOD: Frozen backbone → regression → selective routing
   (not random-restart hill climbing).

3. DIFFERENT CONTRIBUTION: Runtime–quality Pareto with real refinement
   (not objective surface analysis or teacher–student distillation).

4. KEY FRAMING: The optimizer is infrastructure. This paper is deployment intelligence.

5. REAL REFINEMENT: All refinement results use actual PDE + contour scoring.
   This is verified by refinement_protocol.txt and the scored outputs in
   refinement_results.csv.

Limitations:
- N=174 is small; mean baseline competitive with learned models.
- Pseudo-OOD uses batch proxy, not true distribution shift.
- Perturbation-based refinement; gradient-based could be better.

Venue positioning: Plant Phenomics, CVPR workshops, or applications-focused ML venues.
