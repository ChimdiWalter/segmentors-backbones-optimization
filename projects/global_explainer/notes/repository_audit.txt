1  PURPOSE
----------
This repository supports a two-line research programme on lesion phenotyping:

  LINE 1 (OPTIMIZER / CHARACTERIZATION)
    Gradient-free stochastic optimization of Navier-Stokes active contours,
    parameter manifold geometry, ablation studies.

  LINE 2 (DEPLOYMENT / DISTILLATION)
    Zero-shot segmentation via physics-informed knowledge distillation,
    selective semi-amortized inference, full-vs-patch comparison, runtime-
    quality tradeoffs.

Several manuscripts serve both lines simultaneously.

7  STRUCTURAL OBSERVATIONS
================================================================================

a) Naming drift: Paper numbering is sequential but Paper_6 has two versions
   and Paper_7 has three variants. The naming convention is
   Paper_N_<description>_<version>/.

b) Empty placeholders: Paper_7_v2_fromscratch and Paper_8_codex_v1 are 0%
   complete — these should either be populated or removed before submission.

c) Data scale: The experiment outputs exceed 185,000 files. Any CI or
   packaging step must account for this volume.

d) Bibliography fragmentation: refs.bib appears in multiple locations with
   varying entry counts (5-47). A single canonical bibliography would reduce
   maintenance burden.

e) No .gitignore covers the large data/output directories. The git status
   shows many untracked paths.

f) The dataset_segmentation/ subtree contains its own training pipeline
   (dataset.py, run.py, eval.py, checkpoints, SLURM logs) and appears
   semi-independent.

g) Paper_4 (Optimizer) and Paper_5 (Deployment) are the two complete,
   submission-ready manuscripts and represent the primary deliverables.
