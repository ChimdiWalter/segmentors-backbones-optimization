WHAT MAKES THIS PAPER DISTINCT FROM THE PRIOR PAPERS
=====================================================

1. VS THE FULL-LEAF PAPER (Paper_3 / Paper_6)
   - The full-leaf paper only considered downsampled whole-leaf inference
   - This paper adds the patchwise branch and directly compares the two
   - This paper introduces the oracle gap concept (not present in full-leaf paper)
   - The per-parameter local/global decomposition is new
   - External 18.7 evaluation is integrated rather than ad-hoc

2. VS THE PATCH PAPER
   - The patch paper ran patchwise experiments in isolation
   - This paper provides the first matched-split comparison with full-leaf
   - This paper does not oversell patchwise: it honestly reports the oracle gap
     and identifies parameters where full-leaf is superior
   - Aggregation methods are systematically compared (5 methods vs ad-hoc)
   - Heterogeneity analysis is connected to biological interpretation

3. VS THE PRIOR INTEGRATED ATTEMPT
   - The prior attempt combined results from separate runs on different splits
   - This paper builds both branches from scratch on a single authoritative split
   - The error decomposition (prediction + aggregation + oracle gap) is new
   - Real patch ablations with 3 configurations (not interpolated)
   - Statistical testing is more rigorous (paired tests on matched leaves)
   - The paper is substantially longer and more detailed (31 pages vs ~15)

4. UNIQUE CONTRIBUTIONS OF THIS PAPER
   a) Dual oracle formulation with quantified oracle gap
   b) Three-way error decomposition
   c) Per-parameter local vs global classification with physical interpretation
   d) Hybrid approach recommendation based on parameter type
   e) Explicit speedup denominator framework
   f) Complete provenance infrastructure
   g) Failure taxonomy identifying complementary failure modes
